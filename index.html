Fundamentals

What Is a QR Code?

A two-dimensional matrix barcode invented in 1994 by Denso Wave for tracking automotive parts. Today it powers payments, menus, authentication, and ticketing worldwide.

▦

2D Matrix Structure

Grid of black and white square modules arranged in precise patterns to encode information

📐

40 Versions

From Version 1 (21×21) to Version 40 (177×177) scaling with content complexity

💾

Massive Capacity

Up to 7,089 digits or 4,296 alphanumeric characters in a thumbnail-sized space

🛡️

Error Correction

Four levels: L (7%), M (15%), Q (25%), H (30%) allowing reconstruction when damaged

Error Correction

Reed-Solomon: The Magic of Error Correction

Based on Galois field arithmetic (GF(256)), this algorithm reconstructs data even when up to 30% is destroyed. Click modules below to "damage" the QR code and watch it recover.

🧮 Mathematical Foundation

Polynomial interpolation over finite fields (Galois Field GF(2⁸)) enables data reconstruction from partial information.

P(x) = a₀ + a₁x + a₂x² + ... + aₖ₋₁xᵏ⁻¹

🌍 Proven Technology

Also used in CDs, DVDs, Blu-ray discs, deep space transmissions (Voyager), and digital television since the 1960s.

Reverse Process

The Decoding Process

Scanning reverses the encoding pipeline. Click through each decoding step to see how your phone reads a QR code.

📷

1. Detection & Localization

Camera captures image. Finder patterns (the three big squares) are identified using their unique 1:1:3:1:1 ratio. Orientation determined.

🔲

2. Perspective Correction

Homography transformation applied to correct skew, tilt, and distortion from viewing angle.

🔳

3. Grid Sampling

Modules extracted from calibrated grid positions. Timing patterns used to confirm module boundaries.

📋

4. Format Reading

Mask pattern and ECC level extracted from format information bits adjacent to finder patterns.

🎭

5. Unmasking

Applied mask reversed using XOR operation, revealing raw data and error correction modules.

🔧

6. Reed-Solomon Decoding

Data reconstructed, errors detected and corrected using ECC blocks. Damaged modules repaired.

✅

7. Final Extraction

Bitstream decoded according to mode indicator, text/URL delivered to application. Done!
